About Me

I am a data scientist and machine learning engineer with a Master of Science in Data Science from the University of Colorado Boulder. My work spans the full data science lifecycle -- from exploratory analysis and feature engineering to building, deploying, and monitoring production ML models. I am particularly drawn to problems in natural language processing and computer vision, and I enjoy building end-to-end systems that translate research into impact.

During my graduate studies, I developed deep expertise in deep learning architectures, statistical modeling, and MLOps practices. I thrive in environments where I can combine rigorous analytical thinking with hands-on engineering to deliver solutions that work at scale.
