How I Work

Rigorous Validation

Two decades in zero-failure environments taught me that assumptions kill. I validate at every stage — from data quality checks to cross-validated model evaluation.

Clear Communication

The best model is useless if stakeholders can't act on it. I translate complex results into clear, actionable recommendations — a skill refined by years of briefing decision-makers under pressure.

Structured Planning

Every project starts with a plan. I break complex problems into phases, define success criteria upfront, and build in checkpoints. This approach comes directly from military operational planning.

End-to-End Ownership

I don't hand off problems. From data collection to model deployment to stakeholder presentation, I own the full pipeline. That accountability mindset is non-negotiable.
