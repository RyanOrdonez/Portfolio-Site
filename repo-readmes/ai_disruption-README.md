# AI Disruption

**Analyzing the Impact of AI on Industries and the Workforce**

## Status: Planned

> This project is in the planning phase. The goal is to analyze and visualize how AI is disrupting various industries, with a focus on data-driven insights.

## Planned Scope

- Data collection on AI adoption rates across industries
- Analysis of job displacement vs. job creation trends
- Visualization dashboards showing AI impact metrics
- Case studies on specific industry transformations
